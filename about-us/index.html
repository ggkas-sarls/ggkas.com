Our Values

Be Accessible

Our products are designed to be accessible to all people. We strive to bring the highest-end of technological innovation to all. We aim to achieve that by streamlining complexity without any functional reduction.

Be Radical

We embrace failures and embark on a journey to the impossible. We start from a definition of what people need and strive to make it feasible. We achieve that through true, patient, and radical innovation.

Be Helpful

Our products are anchored on daily problems and common needs. With a consumer oriented mindset, our guiding principle is to build what is needed.

About Us

GGkas was founded to apply state-of-the-art AI on everyday  tasks that are accessible to everyone. With a list of projects to build, we started in 2023 on the vision to pave the road for the future.

GGkas strives to bring together highly skilled and highly creative people, putting a strong emphasis on innovation. True innovation is slow. We are building our technological repository step by step. 

Our first product aims to bridge the gap between specialized accounting services and consumers, with a service anyone can use. Modern AI is our key weapon to streamline without simplifying

Georgios Varisteas, PhD – CEO & CTO

Georgios brings over 20 years of experience in software engineering and AI. For the past decade, he has held leadership roles, including a three-year tenure as CTO where he developed a market-ready product and helped raise €330,000 in funding. An accomplished researcher, he has published several award-winning articles on cutting-edge AI in scientific conferences and journals. His technical expertise and visionary leadership drive our mission to simplify personal finance management through advanced technology.

Georgia Kanli – Chief Product Officer (CPO)

Georgia holds two MSc degrees in Applied Physics and has over 10 years of experience as a Research Engineer in medical imaging. She has published research on AI, demonstrating her proficiency in complex data analysis and problem-solving—skills directly applicable to fintech product development. Her expertise ensures our products are both intuitive and engaging for users.
